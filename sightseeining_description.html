TourSummary:
Join this tour and experience the sights of London from the comfort of a modern riverliner and also get a comprehensive guided tour via headphones in a number of languages.

QuickView:
This cruise ticket enables you to cruise along the Thames from Westminster Pier (close to Westminster Abbey) down to The Tower of London on board one of its luxury river liners. See and experience the sights and splendour of this great city from the relaxed comfort of one of City Cruises' modern, all-weather, wheelchair-friendly RiverLiners with open upper decks and spacious lower saloons, with panoramic windows for the best views in town. City Cruises have a unique RiverGuide system which provides a running commentary during your cruise, using GPS technology, in your choice of six languages, English, French,German, Mandarin, Japanese and Spanish.
